Glue Machinery

A responsive Wordpress/Woocommerce store for the Glue Machinery.

View gluemachinery.com

Glue Machinery's old site was klunky and outdated. They needed an upgrade that could easily manage the hundred of industrial glue systems and adhesives products that they manage on the backend and still display everything with an updated look and feel on the front.

Custom Wordpress theme and Woocommerce storefront and checkout process. Developed while working at Duckpin.
