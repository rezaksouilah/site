Black Collar Printing

A responsive Wordpress site for Black Collar Printing

View blackcollarprinting.com

Black Collar Printing needed an upgrade from their previous tumblr blog. They needed a portfolio that is easy to navigate and an order form to keep things organized. A custom responsive wordpress theme did the trick.

I worked directly with Scott Archer, black collar's founder, to design and develop a site that simplified their previous portfolio as well as their job ordering process.
